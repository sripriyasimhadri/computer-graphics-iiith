Projections and Cameras


The Set-up: Bunny through a Camera
This experiment is designed to teach the orthographic and perspective projections using cameras and a bunny. A Camera node is similar to an instance. It has a Coordinate system which is a local coordinate system coordinate system wrt the Global fixed coordinate system. The camera is always placed at the origin of this Coordinate system looking towards X-axis.
A bunny model and a perspective camera are preloaded into this experiment.
The type of camera (orthographic/perspective) can be changed in the camera properties.
